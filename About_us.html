    About Us

    Quick Repair fixes electronic devices (it’s in our name after all). From the classic phone drop in the parking lot to the tablet in the toilet. We’ve seen it all. We understand how traumatic it is when you break your favorite devices and we want you to feel better the moment you walk in.

    Who are we?

    At Quick Repair we’re obsessed with electronic gadgets. We’re the folks standing in line on launch day waiting for the latest smartphone or gaming console. We love all things tech and we’re experts at what we do.

    Why we’re here?

    We’re not overly sentimental, but we do what we do because we care about people and their tech. That’s why Abhi Repair works; we put our customers above anything else. Of course, we love fixing cracked screens and solving the cooling parameters of your air conditioner, etc. but we get our satisfaction from helping out folks who have lost the faith in the service industry.

    How we started?

    As a group of engineers our ultimate goal was to solve problems in the society. So eight engineers joined hands to solve this problem along with the interns with the support of thousands of people both our customers and regular visitors. Hence Abhi Repair was formed on 25th September 2017.

    What we offer?

    From electronic & electrical to automobile service, selling of refurbished laptops and we also infuse accessories sales. We provide high quality service and hence comes with a 30 day warranty. The best part is that you can avail our services at the comfort of your house/office/apartment or you can drop your product to the nearest Abhi Repair service centre.
